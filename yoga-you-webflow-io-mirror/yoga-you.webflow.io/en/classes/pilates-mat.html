About this class

Pilates Mat is for anyone who wants to strengthen their core, improve posture and gain flexibility on the floor, without equipment. In small groups, Souhila Chekara guides each session with precision and care.

What you'll work on

  • Core and stability: deep muscles, engagement and alignment.
  • Breath: coordinating breath and movement, a pillar of the method.
  • Strength and flexibility: complete exercises adapted to all levels.
  • Gentle progress: personalised corrections in a calm setting.

Practical info

  • Duration: 1 hour (ex. mardi 18 h 30 – 19 h 30)
  • Price: €12.50 per class · 5-class Mat pack at €55
  • Level: all levels
  • Booking: on bsport

Contact us
How do I book a class?

Choose your slot on bsport, create an account if needed and confirm online. Reformer spots go quickly: book ahead.

What are the prices?

Reformer: €32 per class, 5- and 10-class packs available. Pilates Mat and Ashtanga Yoga: €12.50 per class. See Pricing or bsport for current plans.

Do I need prior experience?

No for beginner Mat and all-levels slots. Intermediate sessions require a foundation in Pilates or Yoga. Souhila adapts exercises to each participant.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing, non-slip socks for Reformer, water bottle. For outdoor Ashtanga Yoga, bring a mat and weather-appropriate clothing.

Where is the studio?

Studio Pilates Narbonne, 8 Rue du Luxembourg, 11100 Narbonne. Easy access in the city centre.

Can I cancel a booking?

Cancellation terms are those shown on bsport when booking. Questions? Contact us by phone or email.
